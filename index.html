Process

From garment
to gateway.

One NFC chip embedded in your garment becomes the attendee's complete event identity — entry pass, wallet, seat guide, and order terminal in a single seamless tap.

01

NFC Garment Issued

Each attendee receives an NFC-embedded garment pre-loaded with their event profile, wallet balance, and tiered access permissions.

02

Tap to Enter & Navigate

A single tap grants instant access, pushes table assignments and personalised venue navigation directly to the guest's app.

03

Cashless Ordering & Wallet

Guests tap to pay, order food and beverages, top up wallets — entirely cashless via SAMA-compliant payment gateways.

04

Real-Time Intelligence

Operators see live dashboards of guest flows, spend analytics, and crowd heat maps — enabling instant operational decisions at scale.
